Welcome to the Forge

The Forge intends to provide the environment to create a collaborative design community on campus, promoting communication, critical thinking, and STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) ideals. Through this, the Forge will provide easy and everyday access to rapid prototyping tools, communication and collaboration tools, and a casual area to work. The Forge will hold workshops, design competitions, and speakers involved in the Making Movement.

Laser Cutter

We can laser cut or engrave anything short of metal and excluding materials creating known carcinogens (PVC and MDF).  Our software takes ".dxf" files but volunteers can also help you engrave or cut from any image file

3D Scanner

Have an object you want a digital, 3D copy of? If its small enough to fit on our scanner you can get a high quality 3D scan. Just ask one of our volunteers and wear the infared protection glasses.

Vinyl Cutter and Embroidery
